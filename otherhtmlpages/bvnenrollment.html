Biometric Authentication in the Nigerian Banking System

Introduction and Implementation of the Bank Verification Number (BVN) by the Central Bank of Nigeria

The increasing compromise on conventional security systems like passwords and PINs has led to a demand for more secure access to sensitive information in the Banking System. To address this, the Central Bank of Nigeria, in collaboration with all banks, launched the Bank Verification Number (BVN) on February 14, 2014.

BVN is a centralized biometric identification system utilizing human characteristics such as fingerprints and signatures for enhanced real-time security. All bank customers in and out of Nigeria are required to register for BVN, providing a unique and verifiable identifier across the entire Nigerian Banking Industry, even for those with accounts in different banks. This initiative aims to strengthen overall security and streamline authentication processes.

Check BVN

Check Your BVN on Wema Bank

The official USSD code you need to dial to retrieve or check your Bank Verification Number (BVN) on WEMA Bank Nigeria is *565*0#.

Link Your BVN

How to link your BVN to your Wema Bank Account

To link your BVN details to your WEMA bank account using USSD code, simply dial *945*BVN# on your phone and press the send button. Another option available to you is by using the BVN linking portal. In case you want to manage your BVN, the USSD code to dial is *945*4#.
